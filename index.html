Services

The Deduction Group offers an array of services including, but not limited to:

  • business plan writing
  • financial modelling and budgeting
  • market research
  • government funding advice and applications
  • due diligence packages
  • sales and investor pitch development

We also work with local Graphic Design students at Algonquin College to develop high value logo design, website design and marketing collateral.
